# EcoStream Conservation Bio

## Mission
EcoStream Conservation is dedicated to restoring and protecting freshwater ecosystems in the Pacific Northwest. We focus on salmon habitat restoration, water quality monitoring, and community education.

## Current Programs
1. **Salmon Run Revival:** A large-scale project to remove obsolete dams and restore natural river flow.
2. **Citizen Science Water Watch:** Training 500+ volunteers annually to monitor stream health across three counties.
3. **Green Corridor Initiative:** Planting native riparian vegetation to prevent erosion and provide cooling shade for local fish species.

## Financial Background
Founded in 2015, we are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit with an annual operating budget of $1.2M. We primarily receive funding from private foundations and state environmental grants.
